About E-Doctor

Through E-Doctor, patients from all around the world will be capable of accessing and meeting a certified doctor just from the screen in their homes. Instead of going out of your way to visit the doctor's office, you could find the perfect doctor for you, right at your fingertips. To put this simply, patients will be capable of choosing between an assortment of doctors. Once they find the doctor that is most suitable for them, they can schedule online appointments.

How It Works

When it comes to the website, we have offered a variety of functions that the user can access. On the top, are clickable tabs that can direct the user into information that is desired. Moreover, one of the tabs directs the user into where they can schedule an appoint, while another directs them to descriptions on the doctors.

The Specialties We Provide

In regards to our services, we provide a wide variety of doctors that will be able to satisfy your different needs. This includes a professional psychiatrist, dermatologist, general practictioner, and a pediatricion.

Customer Service/Dedication

For our staff and services, we plan to ensure the best treatment towards customers. If there are any questions about the website, our customer service number can be called where someone may assist you.

Scheduling an Appointment

In regards to scheduling an appointment, a patient must first create a login account. Click on "Login" at the top of the website to create an account. After, the patient must choose one of the four doctors above. There will be an option that will allow patients to interact with the doctor that best suits them.
